About

Who we are

Established in 1977, Arora Refractories in India is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high quality refractory products. Founded by a group of technocrats, Arora is now a professionally managed Refractory Company that has grown multifold in business volume and market reach. With more than 3 decades of experience Arora has always enjoyed a reputation of quality products and outstanding service.

Our other product range includes:-

  • Quality control practices that ensure top quality products at extremely competitive prices.
  • We have served large industrial groups with our products and services and as a result gained their valuable experience, respect and patronage over the years.
  • Our environment friendly and energy saving technology will dramatically reduce energy consumption in your processes and reduce damaging emissions to the environment.

Arora Refractories has been serving a variety of clients internationally including Middle East, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Bangladesh , Saudi Arabia and Africa by exporting superior quality refractory materials. Our reputation in the Industry as a Castable manufacturer has made us earn laurels in products such as Insulating castables and low cement castables.

Services

What we do offer

Besides manufacturing and supplying a wide range of refractory products

we provide complete installation services for furnaces, boilers and other similar projects.

Technical Services

Our technical expertise is available to our customers for all their refractory related needs. We offer a variety of technical solutions to most customer specifications for furnaces and boilers.

Consulting and Refractory material selection services

We also offer consulting and refractory material selection services. Our extensive experience in this industry puts us in a solid position to offer valuable consulting and resourcing services.

We cater mainly to the following industries:

  • Ceramic & Chemical industries like sanitary ware, Tiles & Lime industries etc.
  • Glass Plants.
  • Cement Plants.

We cater mainly to the following industries:

  • Iron & Steel industries including Melting, Rolling, Forging & Heat Treatment industries etc.
  • Furnace Manufacturers & Consultants.

We cater mainly to the following industries:

  • Ferrous & Non-Ferrous metal industries.
  • Fertiliser & petrochemical industries.
